Historic Places in my hometown, Nelspruit (Mpumalanga):

Sudwala Caves

Sudwala caves are one of the oldest known caves in the world. At 2800 Million years old, they are recently known for being a fortress for afrikaaners in the mid-1900s and Somquba, Sobhuza I's brother who tried to usurp the throne.

Lonecreek Falls

The lonecreek falls is found in the Sabie river and has been declared a national monument. Local people have long used the waterfall for cleansings and other ceremonies. The waterfall and the river were utilised by the community until the inception of the Land Act of 1913.

Mbombela stadium

The Mbombela stadium is Mpumalanga's first large capacity stadium. It was launched for the 2010 Fifa world cup. The construction of this stadium has brought exciting events in the town, it can even be utilised for exhibitions and matric rewrite classes.
